- **Significance**: Sculptor selected to represent South Korea at the 2026 Venice Biennale (61st edition) in the Korean Pavilion. Won the Frieze Seoul Artist Award in 2024. ### 2. 노혜리 / Hyeree Ro
- **Type**: person
- **Significance**: NY-based Korean performance and installation artist (b. 1987) selected as co-artist for the 2026 Venice Biennale Korean Pavilion alongside Choi Goen. Her practice links objects, bodies, and language to explore embodied narratives. Her Venice work *Bearing* envelops the pavilion in a wax-coated membrane with eight thematic stations, including a Nobel laureate Han Kang contribution. Has participated in Leeum Museum's Art Spectrum 2022 and held recent solo exhibitions in Seoul and New York.

### 3. 김윤신 / Kim Yun Shin
- **Type**: person
- **Significance**: First-generation Korean female sculptor (b. 1935, Wonsan) who spent nearly four decades in Argentina and is now receiving her largest retrospective at Hoam Museum of Art (March–June 2026). A pioneering figure in Korean modernist sculpture known for chainsaw-worked wood pieces and geometric abstraction. Represented in the Guggenheim Museum, New York. Participated in a Venice Biennale 2024 collateral event. Represented by Kukje Gallery (Seoul) and Lehmann Maupin (international). At 91, she is one of Korea's most internationally visible senior artists.

### 4. 유영국 / Yoo Youngkuk
- **Type**: person
- **Significance**: Pioneer of Korean abstract painting (1916–2002), often paired with Kim Whanki as one of the two grand masters of Korean abstraction. Studied at Bunka Gakuin University, Tokyo (1938) and was engaged with Constructivism, Suprematism, and avant-garde movements before returning to Korea. Major retrospective "Yoo Youngkuk: The Mountain is Within Me" at Seoul Museum of Art (SeMA) runs May–October 2026, marking the 110th anniversary of his birth. Represented by Pace Gallery internationally; work on Artsy, Ocula, and Artforum. Had a Venice Biennale 2024 collateral event. Despite being deceased, his institutional significance and ongoing retrospective activity make him a critical lexicon entry.
